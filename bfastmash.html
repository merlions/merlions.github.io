Ed's Breakfast Mash

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Ingredients:

  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Zucchini
  • Broccoli
  • Cauliflower rice, or regular if preferred
  • Butter
  • Soy sauce
  • Kale
  • Spinach
  • Sweet/bell peppers, sliced
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 Egg
  • Hot sauce, to taste

Prepare rice. Put butter and soy sauce into pan. Pan fry onions and garlic until onions are clarified and garlic is lightly browned. Add zucchini, broccoli and rice, and fry until soft. Wilt kale and spinach in separate pan, and fry an egg over medium. Combine rice and kale/spinach mix in a bowl. Add raw bell peppers and tomatoes. Place egg on top and add hot sauce as desired.
