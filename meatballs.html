Joe Giuffre's Meatballs and Gravy

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Meatballs:

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 spoonful of garlic
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 red onion

    • Gravy:

  • 3 large cans crushed plum tomatoes (italian brand, not flavored with basil)
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 12-15 fresh basil leaves
  • 6 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1/2 red onion
  • olive oil
  • 6-8oz of your favorite dark red wine (merlot or chianti)

    • Note: NEVER add parsley

Pour the 3-4 tablespoons of olive oil into a large pot, or just enough to cover the bottom. Put diced red onions and crushed garlic into the pot and lightly brown. Pour in the tomato paste and all 3 cans of crushed plum tomatoes and a can and a half of water, using tomato cans to measure. Let simmer for an hour and add basil. Leave for another half hour, add wine, and leave for another 20 minutes.
