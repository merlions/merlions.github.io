Tata Isabelle's Potato Salad

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Sliced vidalia onion (or other mild onion)
  • Chopped flat parsley
  • Sherry vinegar
  • Olive oil
  • Sea salt

Peel, quarter, and slice potatoes. Steam in pressure cooker for 3 minutes. Add in onion, parsley, sherry vinegar, olive oil, and sea salt.
