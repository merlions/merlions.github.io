Tata's Apple Cider Vinegar and Honey Vinaigrette Dressing

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup olive oil (good quality)
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Combine vinegar, water, honey, salt, and pepper. Drizzle olive oil in while stirring until combined.
