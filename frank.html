Frank Steak

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Marinade:

  • 2/3 cup cooking sherry
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger
  • 1 teaspoon anise seed
  • 1 heaping tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup scallions, cut up

    • 1 flank steak

Tenderize steak by pounding it with a meat hammer. Pour all marinade ingredients into a gallon size ziploc bag and mix. Add steak to bag and coat thoroughly in marinade. Shake or otherwise mix bag to re-coat steak every 20 minutes. Cook meat on grill.

    Gravy:

    Take leftover marinade and mix in 3-4 tablespoons of flour and warm water.

1 steak serves 2-4 people. Doubling the recipe requires each steak having its own bag full of individually made marinade.
