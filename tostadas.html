Alex, Jo, Reyna, Hadley, and Chris's Tostadas

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Ingredients:

  • 2 large or 4 regular cans of diced tomatoes
  • 2 large or 4 regular cans of black beans
  • 1 small can of green chilis
  • Onions, diced
  • Garlic, minced
  • 2tbsp olive, corn, or canola oil
  • Sour cream, if desired
  • Cheese, if desired
  • Tostada or taco shells

Lightly fry garlic and onions in oil in very large pot until brown. Add tomatoes, beans, and chilis, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until thick, about 45 minutes. Spoon mixture onto shells. Top with cheese and/or sour cream if desired. Feeds 10-15 people and keeps for a long time in the fridge. Alternately, you can add rice and eat with a spoon.
