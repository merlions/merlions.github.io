Nana's Chicken Bee

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Ingredients:

  • 5 lbs chicken breasts (fancy) OR 1 whole chicken (cheaper), cooked, cut bite size
  • 2 cans water chestnuts, drained and sliced
  • 2 4oz cans of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 3 cans mushroom soup mix, undiluted
  • 1 small jar of pimentos
  • Black olives, sliced (optional)
  • 1 pkg Pepperidge Farm stuffing
  • 2 sticks of butter or margarine, melted

Mix all ingredients except stuffing and oleo (butter or margarine). Put in greased baking dish - shallow is best. Mix stuffing and melted oleo. Put stuffing mix on top of mix.

Freeze at this point if you wish.

Cook at 350° covered with foil loosely (so stuffing won't burn) til bubbling - 30 to 40 mins.

Serves 10-12.
