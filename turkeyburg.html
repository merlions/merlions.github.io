Uncle Ed's Turkey Burgers

  • Home
  • Vegetarian
  • With Meat
  • Desserts

    Ingredients:

  • Ground turkey
  • Any very stiff, thick artisan bread or oats
  • Dried cranberries (pre-sweetened with orange juice, if you can find them)
  • Half and half mix of wild rice and brown rice
  • 1 egg
  • Diced green granny smith apples
  • Tarragon
  • Rosemary
  • Sage

Combine all ingredients to taste and form patties. Grill or pan fry until cooked through and lightly golden brown on both sides. If you have a meat thermometer make sure the internal temperature is 165°F.

Can also be formed into meatloaf and cooked at 350°F for 13-15 minutes, or until top is golden brown, meat is cooked through, and/or internal temperature reaches 165°F.
